// Known Vulnerabilities

1. Over-Refusals (Safety Guardrails)

Claude can be "too safe." It occasionally refuses to discuss benign technical topics (like cybersecurity testing) due to overly aggressive alignment filters.

2. Spatial Logic Gaps

In 3D modeling or complex physics descriptions, the model still hallucinates relative positions. It is a linguistic genius, but still a spatial novice.

3. Token Limitation Burn

Using the Artifacts window consumes project tokens at a faster rate. High-intensity sessions can lead to account limits being reached in as little as 2 hours.
