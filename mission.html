Transparency & Survival

Maintaining a high-tech testing lab requires capital. To remain sustainable and independent, NextGenStacks participates in various affiliate programs.

Crucial Principle: We never, under any circumstances, accept payment to alter a test result. If we provide an affiliate link, it is because the product passed our rigorous "Survivor Audit".

If a tool fails our tests, we will publish the failure publicly, even if that tool has a high-paying affiliate program. Our loyalty is to the end-user, not the vendor.
