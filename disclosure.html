Disclosure & Editorial Ethics

Transparency is not optional. This page explains how NextGenStacks operates, how we earn revenue, and how we protect editorial independence.

1. Affiliate Relationships

Some links on NextGenStacks are affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you choose to purchase through them — at no additional cost to you.

We currently participate in affiliate programs such as Digistore24 and similar digital marketplaces.

Affiliate relationships do not determine our conclusions.

2. Editorial Independence

All tools, platforms, and systems featured on NextGenStacks are evaluated using direct testing, comparative analysis, and practical workflow observation.

We do not accept payments for positive reviews, rankings, or verdict manipulation.

3. Evidence-First Methodology

Our reviews prioritize measurable outcomes, operational trade-offs, and real-world limitations.

Where estimates, subjective judgments, or lab simulations are used, they are clearly framed as such.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not promote tools solely because they pay higher commissions
  • We do not hide weaknesses or known limitations
  • We do not publish paid “guest verdicts” or sponsored rankings

Editorial decisions are made before monetization is considered — not after.
